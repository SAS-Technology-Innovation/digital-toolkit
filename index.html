Request a New App

Can't find the tool you need? Follow this process to request a new application for the SAS Digital Toolkit.

1 Search the Toolkit
Use the search bar or browse through divisions to ensure a similar tool isn't already available. Try asking the AI Assistant for recommendations.
2 Consult Your Team
Talk to your department lead or PLC coach about your specific requirements. They may know of existing solutions or can help refine your needs.
3 Submit App Request
If no suitable solution exists, submit a formal app request with the information below.
Information Required for App Request
1. What problem or opportunity does this tool seek to address?
2. Which grade level and subject areas will this tool serve and for what purpose (e.g., classroom instruction, homework, assessment, differentiation, communication, data tracking)?
3. How will you measure the impact or success of this tool?
4. What other tools or alternatives have you considered?
5. Do you foresee any training or support needs for this tool? If so, what kind?
6. What is the cost, how many licenses will you need, and who will be using the tool?
