## How to Use This Dashboard

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select Your Division">
    Click on one of the tabs (Whole School, Elementary, Middle School, or High School) to view applications relevant to your division.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Browse Applications">
    Scroll through the "Apps Everyone Can Use" section for applications available to all users in your division.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Explore Department-Specific Apps">
    View applications organized by department to find specialized tools for your subject area.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Access an Application">
    Click on any application name to visit its website and get started.
  </Step>
</Steps>
